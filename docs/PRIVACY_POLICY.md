# Quick Test Cases for Jira - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 26, 2025

---

## Introduction

Quick Test Cases for Jira ("the Service") is a Jira Cloud application developed by Technical Dost. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

---

## Information We Process

When you use Quick Test Cases for Jira, the following information is processed:

- **Atlassian Account Identifier:** Your unique account ID provided by Atlassian, used to record who performed each test run
- **Jira Issue Identifiers:** Issue keys used to associate test cases with specific Jira issues
- **User-Generated Content:** Test status (Pass/Fail/Blocked), test notes, and test run history you create within the Service

---

## How We Use Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

- Store and retrieve test case status and notes within Jira
- Record test run history with tester attribution
- Associate test cases with the correct Jira issues
- Provide the core functionality of the Service

We do NOT use your data for:
- Analytics or tracking
- Advertising or marketing
- Sharing with third parties
- Any purpose other than providing the Service

---

## Data Storage and Security

All data is stored exclusively within **Atlassian's Forge Storage infrastructure**. 

**We do NOT:**
- Store any data in external systems or databases
- Transfer data outside of Atlassian's platform
- Cache data beyond 24 hours
- Copy personal data to our own servers
- Have access to read, export, or share your data

Your test data remains entirely within Atlassian's secure cloud environment. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using Atlassian's enterprise-grade security measures.

---

## Data Retention

- Test case data is stored until you reset the test or the issue is deleted
- When you reset a test case, the history is preserved for audit purposes
- Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data

---

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access:** View test case status and history anytime within the Jira interface
- **Modification:** Update test status and notes at any time
- **Deletion:** Reset test cases using the Reset button
- **Portability:** View test history for manual export

---

## Third-Party Services

This app operates entirely within the Atlassian ecosystem and does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services outside of Atlassian's infrastructure.

---

## Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
