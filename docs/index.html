‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored exclusively in Atlassian Forge Storage, within Atlassian's secure cloud infrastructure. We do not use any external servers or databases. Your data never leaves Atlassian.

Can the developer access my data?

No. Forge's architecture ensures that app developers cannot access customer data. We have no ability to view, retrieve, or access any data stored by this App in your Jira instance.

Is this app GDPR compliant?

Yes. Because we use Atlassian Forge Storage, your data benefits from Atlassian's enterprise-grade compliance, including GDPR, CCPA, SOC 2, and ISO 27001.

How do I delete my data?

You can use the "Reset" function in the App to clear test data for a specific issue. Uninstalling the App will remove all app-specific data according to Atlassian's data retention policies.

Is this app free?

Yes! Quick Test Management is completely free for all team sizes. No hidden costs or premium tiers.
