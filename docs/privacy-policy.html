Privacy Policy

App Name: Quick Test Management for Jira

Developer: Technical Dost (Siditude Apps)

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

1. Overview

Quick Test Management for Jira ("the App") is a Forge app designed to help users track test statuses directly within Jira issues. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

2. Data Collection and Storage

The App stores the following data:

  • Test Status: The current status (Pass, Fail, Blocked, Untested, In Progress) of a test case linked to a Jira issue.
  • Test Notes: Optional notes entered by the user regarding the test case.
  • History Logs: Timestamps and status changes for audit history.

All data is stored securely within Atlassian's Forge Storage. We do NOT maintain any external servers or databases. Your data never leaves the Atlassian cloud environment.

3. Data Access

The data stored by the App is accessible only to:

  • Users with access to the specific Jira issue where the data was created.
  • The App's code running within your Jira instance.

The developer (Technical Dost) does not have access to your stored data.

4. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Since all data is stored within Atlassian's infrastructure, the App relies on Atlassian's compliance controls. You have full control over your data:

  • Right to Access: You can view all stored data directly in the App panel.
  • Right to Deletion: Uninstalling the App or deleting the Jira issue will typically handle data according to Atlassian's retention policies.
